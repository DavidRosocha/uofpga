Who We Are

uoFPGA is the University of Ottawa's official Digital Design Club, aiming to teach students who are passionate about FPGAs, hardware description languages, and digital logic.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between theory and practice by providing hands-on experience through tutorials, team projects, and competitions. Whether you're just starting out or looking to push boundaries, we're here to support you.
